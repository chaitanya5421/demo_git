Director's Message

Chartered Accountancy is a well reputed profession in the present day scenario. The demand for this course continues to be stable for the past, present and future years. This is because of its dynamism and rapid changes in the economy. This profession is attracting a lot of young professionals especially the female sector globally, thanks to the corporates. Chartered Accountants are the ones who are specialised in accountancy, auditing and taxation which are a few to say about.


Chartered Accountancy ( CA) is a professional course in Accounting introduced in our country in 1949 with the enactment of The Chartered Accountants Act. The governing body of this course is named as The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. CA program structure currently has three sections, the first one being Common Proficiency Test (CPT), the second one being The Integrated Professional Competency Course (IPCC) and the last being FINAL examination.
